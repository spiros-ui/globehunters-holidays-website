    Your Money Is Safe

    Book With Confidence

    By booking your travel arrangements with Globehunters, you can be rest assured that your money is totally safe. We are ATOL Protected and IATA members. All the package and Flight-Plus holidays we sell are covered by a scheme protecting your money if the supplier fails. We also have comprehensive airline failure insurance so all your flights booked through Globehunters protect customers' money should an airline cease trading. Sales of single components are unlikely to be covered by the ATOL license unless the supplier's terms and conditions make this clear.

    We offer flights, accommodation, package holidays, tours, car rental and travel services to every corner of the globe. We can also tailor-make your holiday whether you are looking for city break, all inclusive package holiday, multi-centre holiday in the USA or a special holiday on a luxury island in the Maldives. Whatever holiday you are looking for we are sure we have it.

    To ensure you receive the best value holiday with great service, we have established close relationships with our travel partners including airlines, luxury hotels and resorts, boutique hotels, local tour companies and many car hire companies throughout the world. Moreover, by selling holidays directly to the public, you can be assured that Globehunters offers competitively priced holidays, flights and accommodation to our customers.

    We believe the key to successful holiday planning lies in the product knowledge of our friendly, experienced and well travelled consultants. Our consultants are able to pass on their first hand experiences of the destinations we feature to ensure you get the best out of your chosen holiday and make your holiday an experience to remember.
